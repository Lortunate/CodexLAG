# Design Context

## Product

CodexLAG is a local-first desktop operator console for managing a single-machine AI gateway. It exposes provider accounts, relays, platform keys, routing policy, diagnostics, and request-level explainability for technical operators.

## Audience

- Primary users are programmers running and debugging provider access on their own machine.
- They need fast visibility into auth health, routing choices, degraded state, diagnostics, and usage without leaving the desktop app.

## Core Jobs

- Onboard and manage official and API-key-backed providers.
- Compare capabilities and account health across providers.
- Author routing policy and understand why a request did or did not route.
- Inspect diagnostics, request history, and recovery paths when something degrades.

## Tone

- Cold
- Precise
- Professional

## Visual Direction

Build the UI like an operator workbench rather than a marketing dashboard:

- Dense but legible
- Calm, steel-toned dark surfaces
- Strong information hierarchy
- High trust, low ornament
- Explainability and system state should feel immediate and inspectable
